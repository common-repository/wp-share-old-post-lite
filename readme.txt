WP Share Old Post plugin that helps you to keeps your old posts alive by publishing them and driving more traffic to them from Facebook and Twitter.
== Description ==

Share automatically your old WordPress posts on Facebook and Twitter! 
Save time and money by making automatic the sharing of previous posts on social networks. With Share Old Post setting in few minutes the posts you want to share and set the frequency of shares on your Facebook and Twitter accounts.
You can, for example, decide that posts are shared randomly every 4 hours and this is 24/7!
You have a lot of work and many tasks to do? Assign to Share Old Post the management of shares on social networks to give the possibility to your fans to discover at any time your website and make increase your traffic from social networks.


Details: 
Automatic and random share of your old posts.
Share your old posts on your facebook and Twitter accounts / pages.
Select the frequency (in hours) of shares.
Select the categories to exclude from automatic sharing.
Set the minimum and the maximum age for posts to be shared.
You can define a text that will introduce the post when sharing on Twitter.

== Installation ==

1. Upload ths directory`wp share old post lite` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
